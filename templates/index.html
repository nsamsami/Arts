Bridging the Arts or BtheArts is a unique collaboration between the

University of California, Berkeley and local K-12 school communities including teachers, students and parents.

Through expanding access to the arts, we provide students with the tools for critical investigation and analysis. These critical thinking strategies help prepare students to succeed both in and beyond the school environment.

BtheArts assists UC Berkeley arts majors in

developing age-appropriate and culturally-sensitive arts curricula

gaining practical teaching experience

BtheArts provides K-12 teachers, students and parents with

new opportunities in sequential arts learning

lessons in a variety of art disciplines

resources for continued arts learning at home
