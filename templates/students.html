Dance, Visual Art, Music, Theater, Performance Art, Spoken Word, Social Practice

As a student instructor with Bridging the Arts you have the opportunity to share your art practice with local elementary school students.

Through Bridging the Arts, you will develop an arts curriculum that explores the essence of your discipline. Over the course of the semester, you will develop ten lesson plans that help your students access and explore the artistic process.
